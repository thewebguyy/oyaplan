# OyaPlan Twitter Launch Playbook 🚀

This is your step-by-step guide to the OyaPlan Twitter launch. Follow this exactly to turn the "group chat wahala" into 1,000+ plan requests in 14 days.

---

## 1. Before You Post Anything (Checklist)
- [ ] **Production URL is Live:** Verify `oyaplan.com` is loading smoothly.
- [ ] **Spot Coverage:** Supabase production database is seeded with at least 25 verified spots (Focus: Ikeja, VI, Lekki).
- [ ] **Environment Variables:** All secrets (Supabase Key/URL) are confirmed in Vercel production.
- [ ] **Social Preview:** OG images render correctly (Test at [Twitter Card Validator](https://cards-dev.twitter.com/validator)).
- [ ] **Tester Feedback:** The `/feedback` URL is shared with 7 initial testers.
- [ ] **Admin Dashboard:** Accessible at `/admin` (Ensure your key is ready).

---

## 2. Day 1 Post (The Announcement)

**Tweet Content:**
> Lagos group chats are where outing ideas go to die.
>
> "What's the plan?" 
> "How much is it?" 
> "Who is coming from where?" 
>
> I built OyaPlan to end the wahala. 4 simple fields, 3 seconds, one complete plan you can just copy to WhatsApp and go.
>
> Try it: https://oyaplan.com

**Attachment:** 
Attach a screenshot of a generated plan: 
- **Setup:** Chill vibe, 3 people, coming from Surulere, ₦30,000 budget. 
- **Why:** This is the most relatable "mid-range" plan for the core audience.

---

## 3. Days 2–7 Content Ideas

- **Day 2: The Price Comparison.** Post a screenshot showing the "Landed Cost" difference between a Mainland (Ikeja) outing vs Island (VI) for the same vibe.
- **Day 3: The ₦15k Challenge.** Share a plan that proves you can still have a great time for ₦15,000 per person in Lagos. Link to `/explore`.
- **Day 4: Group Chat Wahala Quote-Tweet.** Find a viral tweet about group chats not being able to agree on a spot. Reply with a generated plan for their area.
- **Day 5: The "Hidden Gem" Feature.** Showcase a spot in the database that most people don't know about yet. Link to its area in `/explore`.
- **Day 6: User Social Proof.** Share a screenshot of someone saying "OyaPlan saved us today." (Check `/feedback` for quotes).
- **Day 7: The Feedback Loop.** Ask: "What area is missing?" and tell them to suggest spots directly on the results page.

---

## 4. What NOT to Post
- **No generic startup speak:** Avoid "We're disrupting the leisure industry." Say "We're fixing group chats."
- **No "Coming Soon" features:** Only talk about what people can use right now.
- **No stale data:** If a price in the database hasn't been verified recently, don't feature that spot in a tweet.

---

## 5. How to Handle the First 100 Users

- **User says "Price is wrong":** Reply: "Lagos prices change faster than dollar rate! Thanks for flagging — fixed it in the DB now. 🤝"
- **User requests a missing spot:** Reply: "Oya! Adding it to the pipeline. You can also suggest it directly on the results page so the squad sees it faster."
- **User reports a bug:** Reply: "Lagos traffic hit the server hard. Refresh now — we've cleared the road. ⚡"

---

## 6. The @lagosonabudget Play

Wait until **Day 7** and only after at least one organic retweet from someone with 1k+ followers.

**DM Template:**
> "Oya! Love what you do with @lagosonabudget. I built a tool called OyaPlan that helps squads plan their entire link-up (transport + food) based on their budget. Would love for you to give it a spin: https://oyaplan.com"

**Note:** Be brief. Don't ask for a partnership. Let the product do the talking.
